Resume Writing Tips

Expert advice to help you create a standout resume

1. Tailor Your Resume

Customize your resume for each job application. Highlight the skills and experiences most relevant to the position.

2. Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs to demonstrate your achievements and responsibilities.

3. Include Keywords

Use industry-specific keywords that match the job description to help your resume pass through applicant tracking systems.
